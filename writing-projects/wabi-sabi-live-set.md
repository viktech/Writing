# WABI-SABI — Live Set: "Beautiful Decay"

## Act I: Awakening

**Dust & Light** — *Ambient DnB, 170bpm*  
The opener. Granular textures fade in like sunrise through dirty glass. Gentle rolling breaks ease the crowd in. Sets the tone: this set has soul.

**Fading Ink** — *Liquid DnB, 172bpm*  
Melody enters. Watercolor synths and brush-stroke percussion pick up the emotional thread. The audience starts nodding.

**Patina** — *Atmospheric DnB, 172bpm*  
Same tempo, deeper layers. Field recordings blend in, the world gets textured. Patience rewarded with a slow-burning build.

---

## Act II: Descent

**Moss on Stone** — *Jungle/DnB hybrid, 170bpm*  
First real punch. Chopped amens hit, the bass gets primal. The crowd locks in.

**Cracked Glaze** — *Liquid DnB, 174bpm*  
A breath after the jungle chaos — warm but with visible fractures. Emotional peak. The track that makes people close their eyes.

**Worn Thread** — *Halftime drop, 87bpm feel*  
The set halves tempo. Dusty, lo-fi, intimate. A deliberate pause in the storm. Tension builds through restraint.

---

## Act III: Rupture

**Uneven Ground** — *Halftime DnB, 87bpm feel*  
Stays low but gets darker, heavier. Lurching sub bass, industrial grit. The floor shakes.

**Ash Garden** — *Dark DnB, 174bpm*  
The tempo doubles back up — the biggest energy shift in the set. Scorched distortion, heavy neuro bass. Catharsis.

**Kintsugi Bass** — *Neurofunk, 176bpm*  
Peak intensity. The bass breaks apart and rebuilds mid-phrase. Technical, relentless, golden. The climax.

---

## Act IV: Acceptance

**The Imperfect Signal** — *Techstep DnB, 174bpm*  
Pulls back just slightly from the neurofunk peak but stays commanding. Corrupted transmissions fade in and out. The final message: perfection was never the point. The signal degrades, the set ends in static and silence.

---

## Arc Summary

**Soft → Emotional → Primal → Intimate pause → Dark rebuild → Peak fury → Graceful decay**

The wabi-sabi philosophy lived as a rhythm journey.
