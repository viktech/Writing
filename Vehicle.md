## THE CRITICAL SAFETY FACTOR

### Current Situation: E-Scooter Risks
The Nami Burn E 2 Max is dangerous in:
- **Wind** - Unstable, can blow into traffic
- **Cold** - Battery performance degrades, hypothermia risk  
- **Dark** - Poor visibility to other drivers  
- **Winter** - Completely unusable

### Motorcycle Reality: Same Problems Persist
A motorcycle does NOT solve the fundamental safety issues:
- **Wind** - Still exposed, blown around like e-scooter  
- **Cold** - Even with gear, dangerous below 40°F  
- **Dark** - Only slightly better visibility than e-scooter  
- **Winter** - COMPLETELY UNUSABLE on ice/snow  

**Result:** You'd need backup transportation anyway, negating the cost savings.

### Car Solution: Complete Safety Upgrade
- **Enclosed cabin** - Protected from all elements  
- **Climate control** - Safe operation in any temperature  
- **Headlights/taillights** - Maximum visibility  
- **All-season capability** - Drive 365 days/year  
- **Structural protection** - Airbags, crumple zones, rollover protection
